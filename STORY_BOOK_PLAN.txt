--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
0. PRODUCT VISION — what this is
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
A parent or grandparent picks a story (classic fable like "The Tortoise and
the Hare" / "Union is Strength" / "The Crow and the Pitcher" — OR an original
plot Sparky helps shape) and gets a printable picture book where:

  - Every page is FULL COLOR (vibrant flat cartoon, hand-illustrated feel)
  - Same characters appear consistently across all pages
  - Each scene shows characters with SPEECH BUBBLES containing dialogue
    (e.g. "Hi pig! What are you doing here?" / "Just rolling in the mud!")
  - Pages are DOUBLE-SIDED (color print uses both sides — no blank backs
    like coloring books need)
  - Output: a KDP-ready color paperback (standard or premium color)

Use cases:
  - Bedtime read-aloud books
  - Birthday gifts personalized with a child's name as a character
  - Teachers / homeschool moral stories with classic fables
  - Multi-language editions (English first, Hindi/Tamil later)

1. CRITICAL DIFFERENCES vs CURRENT COLORING BOOK
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                    COLORING BOOK              STORY BOOK
                    ─────────────              ────────────
Color               B&W line art               Full color (vibrant flat cartoon)
Sides               Single-sided + blanks      Double-sided (uses both sides)
Border              CSS overlay border         Full-bleed, NO border
Subject size        65-80% (room to color)     Variable — composition-driven
Background          Minimal (color-friendly)   Rich (sets scene mood)
Text on pages       NONE (no letters allowed)  Speech bubbles + optional narration
Page count          20-50 pages                12-32 pages typical
Trim size           8.5×11" (activity book)    6×9" or 8.5×8.5" (picture book)
Paper weight        white 60lb                 premium 70lb white
Royalty/copy        ~$3.50 per book            ~$2-3 per book (color cost higher)
List price          $5.99-7.99                 $9.99-14.99
Audience            Kids who color             Kids who get read TO
Buyer               Parent for craft activity  Parent for bedtime / gift
Time to make        ~3 min for 20 pages        ~5 min for 16 pages

Single most disruptive change: SPEECH BUBBLES with text. Gemini handling
text inside images is the riskiest part of this build.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. SPEECH BUBBLE STRATEGY (the hard part)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
There are two viable approaches. We should ship Approach A first, fall back
to Approach B if quality is unacceptable.

APPROACH A — Gemini renders text inside speech bubbles
  Pros:
    - One image per page (simple)
    - Speech bubble shape, position, character pointer all "free"
    - Looks more natural and integrated
  Cons:
    - Gemini's text rendering is SOMETIMES mangled (typos, missing letters,
      kerning glitches, fake-looking letterforms)
    - Long dialogue (>10 words) often fails
    - Multi-bubble pages (two characters talking) harder to control
  Safety:
    - Vision quality gate verifies spelling — auto-regenerate if failed
    - Cap dialogue at 12 words per bubble, 2 bubbles per page

APPROACH B — Generate scene WITHOUT text, then COMPOSITE bubbles ourselves
  Pros:
    - 100% accurate spelling (text rendered by browser/canvas)
    - Bubble shapes consistent
    - Can change/edit dialogue without regenerating image
  Cons:
    - More moving parts: bubble shape library, font choice, pointer
      direction, white-space detection (don't cover characters' faces)
    - Composition step in pipeline adds engineering complexity
    - Scene must leave room for bubbles → AI prompt must reserve that space

Recommendation: SHIP A FIRST.
  - Ship with quality gate to catch mangled text
  - Track regen rate; if >25% need regen, build B
  - Long-term: B is the right answer for production-grade output


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. AI PROMPT STRUCTURE (story book pages)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Master prompt for each scene must include:

  IDENTITY (header):
    "Children's storybook illustration page, double-sided color paperback,
     full-bleed, KDP printable at 300 DPI, professional picture-book quality."

  STYLE (consistent across all pages of one book):
    "Flat 2D cartoon style. Vibrant flat colors with bold outlines. Soft
     warm palette. Friendly rounded characters with big expressive eyes.
     No realistic shading, no gradients (unless the cover style)."

  CHARACTERS (locked at finalize_brief, repeated per page):
    "Tilly the small green tortoise with a mossy round shell. Hopper the
     tall lanky hare with one floppy ear and a striped scarf."
    (Inject EVERY character descriptor in every page subject.)

  SCENE (page-specific):
    "Tilly is standing at the chalk start line in a sunny meadow with
     daisies and a wooden fence behind her, looking determined and
     focused. Hopper is laughing arrogantly nearby with hands on hips."

  DIALOGUE (1-2 speech bubbles per page, max 12 words each):
    Bubble 1 — speaker: Hopper, text: "I'm going to win, slowpoke!"
    Bubble 2 — speaker: Tilly,  text: "We'll see, Hopper."

  COMPOSITION:
    "Full-bleed illustration filling the entire page, no border, no white
     edge. Speech bubbles positioned with clear tails pointing to each
     speaker's mouth, using readable rounded sans-serif handwriting,
     spelled exactly as given."

  COLOR PALETTE LOCK:
    "Use only this palette across all pages: sky blue (#9DD9F3), grass
     green (#A8D9A0), warm cream (#FFF4D9), brick red (#E15A4F),
     sunshine yellow (#FFC93C), soft brown (#A57C52)."
    (Pick palette per book at finalize_brief, lock for consistency.)

  ABSOLUTE GUARDRAILS:
    - No copyrighted characters (Disney, Pixar, branded)
    - No author name, no URL, no page number drawn IN the image
    - All character anatomy correct (right number of limbs, eyes, etc.)
    - Spelling in speech bubbles exactly as given (CRITICAL)


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. PDF LAYOUT — double-sided color story book
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Different from coloring book PDF:

  Trim:           6×9 inches (default) OR 8.5×8.5 inches (square)
                  Sized at 6×9 = 432×648 points in pdf-lib
  Bleed:          0.125 inch on each edge (1/8")
  Margins:        none — full bleed printing
  Page count:     12 / 16 / 24 / 32 pages (must be multiple of 4 for KDP)
  Color profile:  sRGB → KDP converts to CMYK at print time
  Paper:          standard color OR premium color (user choice; premium = 70lb)

Layout pattern:
  Page 1   = Front cover (full bleed, color, character-driven, big title)
  Page 2   = Title page (interior — title centered, optional dedication)
  Page 3   = Story scene 1 (full color, full bleed, with speech bubbles)
  Page 4   = Story scene 2
  Page 5   = Story scene 3
  ...
  Page n-1 = Final scene (resolution / "happily ever after")
  Page n   = Back cover (full bleed, color, with bottom-right barcode safe-zone)

NO blank pages between scenes (color print uses both sides).
NO uniform black border overlay (unlike coloring book — story books
                                  are full-bleed art).
Page numbers are OPTIONAL and small (lower outer corner if desired).


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. CHARACTER CONSISTENCY — multi-page narrative
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
THIS IS THE #1 QUALITY KILLER for story books. Solved by 4 reinforcing
mechanisms:

  (a) LOCKED DESCRIPTORS at finalize_brief
      Sparky outputs 1-3 characters with detailed descriptors, e.g.
      "Tilly: small green tortoise with mossy round shell, brown determined
              eyes, no clothes."
      "Hopper: tall lanky hare with one floppy ear, brown fur, striped
               red+white scarf."

  (b) INLINE REPETITION in every page subject
      Each scene's subject re-states EVERY character descriptor verbatim
      (just like current Story mode does for B&W).

  (c) REFERENCE-IMAGE CHAINING
      Generate page 1. Use page 1 as STYLE REFERENCE for page 2 generation.
      Use page 1 + 2 as references for page 3. Etc.
      Tradeoff: more API tokens per page; but consistency rises ~30-40%.

  (d) POST-GEN VISION CHECK
      For every page, GPT-4o-mini Vision scores:
        characters_on_screen_match_descriptors? (Y/N)
        palette_matches_locked_palette?         (Y/N)
        characters_consistent_with_page_1?      (Y/N — pass page 1 too)
      If any false → auto-regenerate (1 retry).

7. NEW + CHANGED SCHEMA
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Existing BookBrief:
  { name, icon, coverScene, pageScene, prompts: [{name, subject}] }

Add for story books:
  bookType: "coloring-book" | "story-book"  (default coloring-book for back-compat)
  characters?: Array<{ name: string, descriptor: string }>
  palette?: { name: string, hexes: string[] }
  trim?: "8.5x11" | "6x9" | "8.5x8.5"  (defaults: coloring=8.5x11, story=6x9)

Per scene (replaces flat prompts):
  scenes: Array<{
    name: string
    subject: string         (descriptor of the visible scene)
    dialogue?: Array<{ speaker: string, text: string }>
    composition?: string    (optional camera/framing hint)
  }>

For backward compat: keep `prompts` for coloring books; story books use
`scenes` instead. Or unify (`prompts` shape extended optionally).

9. KDP COLOR PRINT REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Image format:   sRGB (KDP converts to CMYK; do not pre-convert)
  DPI:            300+
  Bleed:          0.125" outside trim on all four sides
  Trim sizes
    accepted:     6×9" (1538×2304 px @ 300 DPI),
                  8.5×8.5" (square),
                  8×10", 7×10", 5×8", many more
  Cover wrap:     spine width depends on page count + paper:
                    standard color: pages × 0.002347"
                    premium color:  pages × 0.0024"
                  Spine includes title text vertical (book ≥80 pages)
  Royalty calc:
    royalty = (list_price × 0.60) − print_cost
    e.g. $9.99 list, 16-page premium 6×9: ~$3.65 print → ~$2.34 royalty
    e.g. $12.99 list, 24-page premium: ~$4.85 print → ~$2.94 royalty
  Categories:
    "Children's Books > Animals", "Bedtime & Dreams", "Friendship",
    "Classic Tales", "Folktales & Myths", "Moral Stories"
  Keywords (different from coloring books):
    "bedtime story for kids", "moral story picture book", "Aesop fable
    illustrated", "kids 3-7 picture book", "read aloud children"

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Suggested CrayonSparks pricing tiers for Story Book product:
  STARTER plan ($9.99/mo): 2 story books / month
  PRO plan ($24.99/mo):    8 story books / month + premium cover style
  STUDIO plan ($49.99/mo): unlimited + character licensing rights

Per-book AI cost (rough):
  - 16 page generations × $0.04 = $0.64
  - 1 front cover + 1 back cover × $0.04 = $0.08
  - Sparky chat ~$0.001
  - Quality gates ~$0.002
  - TOTAL: ~$0.73 per book in raw AI cost
  - Margin against $9.99 royalty: still ~$1.30+ even at the cheapest list
